Welcome!

EventManager is a simple webapp to manage festivals/events. The application is split into four different parts: artist management, band management, podium/stage management and performance management. The codebase is object-oriented and easy to extend, feel free to commit your work. EventManager uses a combination of sqlite3 and Jinja2/Flask to serve its functionality.
